USC Formula Electric Merch

Performance-inspired teamwear where aerodynamic cuts and circuit-tracing graphics ignite team spirit and technical pride.

Overview

This custom merch was designed to spark pride and cohesion among the USC Formula Electric team. Short-sleeve competition tees embrace aerodynamic cuts and color-block panels inspired by racecar bodywork, while zip-up jackets go full throttle with blueprint-style circuit tracings of our electrical system.
